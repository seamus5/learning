Make your event's photos relevant by delivering them live.
No one is looking at event photos 3 days after the event. Eventful speeds up the process by uploading your photos while they are being taken.

  • Put it in your Pocket and Forget it

    Each Eventful device comes paired with a wifi SD card for your photographer. Insert it into your camera and turn on the device to watch your camera, sync with the device and the cloud. Place the device in your pocket and shoot the event.

  • Upload Images

    As the photographer shoots the race the images are resized on the camera and uploaded to the cloud. Once successfully uploaded the image is removed from the SD card so you can shoot all day without running out of memory.

  • Works With Your Matching Solution

    We play nice with all matching solutions out there. Eventful gets your photos to the cloud, how you match and deliver them is up to you.

  • Easy Clean Up

    When the event is over your photographers simply return the Eventful devices to you. Eventful minimizes your post event processing. You've got enough to do, let us handle your images.

The Eventful image upload kit includes a wifi SD card, and a unique android device configured that only runs Eventful's patent pending upload technology.
